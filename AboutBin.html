Bins in public areas

London, United Kingdom

For those who live in block, their waste and recycling materials are collected from relatively bigger bins than normal wheelie bins.

Florence, Italy

Bins have been put on the street for citizens and tourists, and they are divided by different lid colours. For more detail, see the official guide.

Nice, France

Instead of bins, transparent plastic bags are adopted in Nice, and many other cities in Europe.

Since the 1995 terror bombings in France, bins have been replaced by bags, so as to make it more difficult to hide a bomb.

This infographic shows how to recycle in France.

Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona is a modern city, in which it is hard to find individual houses other than high-rise buildings.

According to the government, all citizens have recycling collection containers located less than 100 meters from their home, and they are divided by five colours for different types of materials.
