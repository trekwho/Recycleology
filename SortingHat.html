Which house will you go to if Hogwarts teaches recycling?

One day, when Hogwarts starts to teach recycling, which house will be your home? Well, neither you or me own Sorting Hat, but this quiz might give you hints! Ready? Fasten your seatbelt, let's go.

Hufflepuff

Hufflepuff values dedication, patience and loyalty. When it comes to recycle, you will always try your best to do it, because you regard recycling as such a great action to save out planet. Moreover, you are a good advisor for people who seek help, and you might even volunteer to promote recycling outside your living area. However, sometimes even yourself feel not sure about how to recycle correctly.

Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw values intelligence, knowledge and wit. When it comes to recycle, you will always look for useful information and gracefully apply it into life. You clearly understand the purpose and process of recycling, and you don’t mind explaining to friends. However, sometimes you may miss the chance to help – you think it’s easy for everyone, but the fact goes against your thought.

Slytherin

Slytherin values ambition, cunning and resourcefulness. When it comes to recycle, you know how to do it well and also, you know how to make use of it to upgrade your personal image. It’s not that you don’t actually care about recycling, it’s just you want to gain more from this topic. You will be happy when you receive gratitude from people after helping them, but you won’t initiate communication when you feel it’s not necessary.

Gryffindor

Gryffindor values bravery, daring, nerve, and chivalry. When it comes to recycle, you tend to participate actively, with a sense of justice and pride. You like helping people in need, and you won’t hesitate to challenge others who don’t care about the environment. However, sometimes you might get too confident with yourself, and then just ignore existing rules and follow your own principles.
